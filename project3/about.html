About Ebenezer Hotel

Discover our story, values, and the people behind the warmth

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Ebenezer Hotel began as a family dream to create a sanctuary where guests feel at home. Nestled in the heart of Kigali, we combine modern comfort with authentic Rwandan hospitality.

What started as a small guesthouse has grown into a beloved destination for travelers and food lovers. Our name "Ebenezer" means "stone of help" – a reminder that we are here to serve and uplift every visitor.

Today, we are proud to offer exquisite dining, serene rooms, and memorable events. Every dish is crafted with love, every room designed for peace.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality through genuine care, fresh cuisine, and a relaxing environment that feels like a second home.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s most beloved boutique hotel, where every guest leaves with a smile and a story to tell.
